# Shared Palette Guidance

Use a restrained palette with clear value separation for readability at distance.

## Base Palette
- Primary cloth: desaturated teal or slate
- Armor: warm gray metal with subtle edge wear
- Leather: dark brown
- Accent: amber or muted red for focal points

## Emissive
- Low intensity, narrow bands or small tech nodes
- Keep emissive areas under 5% of surface area

## Material Response
- Cloth: roughness 0.7-0.9
- Leather: roughness 0.45-0.6
- Metal: metallic 1.0, roughness 0.2-0.4
