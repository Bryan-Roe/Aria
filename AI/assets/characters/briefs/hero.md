# Hero Brief

## Silhouette
- Confident, upright posture with broad shoulders and narrow waist
- Iconic cape or long coat to read at distance

## Gear
- Lightweight armor plates, layered cloth, utility belt
- Signature weapon holstered (sword or energy baton)

## Materials
- Cloth (matte), metal trims (medium roughness), leather straps
- Accent color banding for readability

## Style Notes
- Proportions slightly heroic: long legs, larger hands/feet
- Face: simple planes, strong brow, clean stylized nose

## Technical
- LOD0: 18k-22k tris
- LOD1: 9k-12k tris (optional)

## Palette
- Primary: slate cloth
- Accent: muted red
- Metal: warm gray
