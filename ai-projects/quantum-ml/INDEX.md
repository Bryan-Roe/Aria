**Run simulations**

→ Run: `examples/run_simulations.py`

**Train ML models**
→ Read: `DEMONSTRATION_SUMMARY.md` (ML section)
→ Run: `examples/train_models.py`

**Deploy to Azure**
→ Read: `azure/DEPLOYMENT.md`
→ Configure: `config/quantum_config.yaml`
→ Deploy: Follow deployment steps

**Use MCP server**
→ Read: `MCP_SERVER_README.md`
