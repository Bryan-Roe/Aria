# Bruiser Brief

## Silhouette
- Heavy, bulky silhouette with wide torso and thick arms
- Low center of gravity for power

## Gear
- Chunky armor plates, reinforced boots, heavy gauntlets
- Large melee weapon or shield

## Materials
- Metal and composite armor with worn edges
- Minimal cloth, emphasis on solid mass

## Style Notes
- Exaggerated upper body and fists
- Helmet or mask to simplify facial detail

## Technical
- LOD0: 20k-25k tris
- LOD1: 10k-14k tris (optional)

## Palette
- Primary: dark slate
- Accent: muted orange
- Metal: gunmetal
