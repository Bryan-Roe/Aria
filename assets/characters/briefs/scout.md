# Scout Brief

## Silhouette
- Lean, agile, forward-leaning pose
- Slim profile with long limbs for speed

## Gear
- Light backpack, small gadgets, short cloak or scarf
- Compact weapon (bow, pistol, or compact rifle)

## Materials
- Predominantly cloth and lightweight armor
- Subtle emissive accents for tech elements

## Style Notes
- Exaggerated ankle and wrist shapes for read
- Simplified facial features, expressive eyes

## Technical
- LOD0: 15k-18k tris
- LOD1: 7k-10k tris (optional)

## Palette
- Primary: desaturated teal
- Accent: amber
- Metal: cool gray
